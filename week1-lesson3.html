Week 1 - Lesson 3: Division of Powers & the Washminster System

Main Content (Deborah-Led)

Australia has a federal system with powers divided between federal and state governments. Our "Washminster" system blends the British Westminster model with American federal features for stability and checks and balances.

🐝 Deborah says: "This practical system gives us strong leadership while protecting against too much power in one place."
